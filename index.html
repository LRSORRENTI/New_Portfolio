About me

I'm an experienced Web Developer and UX Designer specializing in custom websites that prioritize SEO, responsive design, and accessibility. Proficient in Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, WordPress, Python, and SQL.

I collaborate with clients to deliver tailored solutions that improve online visibility and user engagement. Certified in Advanced Web Development, Full-Stack Web Development, SQL, and UX/UI Design, with a strong focus on modern web technologies and best practices.

My job is to build your website so that it's functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add an innovative touch to your product and make sure that it's eye-catching, aesthetically pleasing, and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and brand identity in the most creative way possible.
